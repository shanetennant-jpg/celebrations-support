Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Celebrations handles your information.

Short version: All data you enter into Celebrations stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

Celebrations does not collect any information from you. The app does not have servers, does not require an account, and does not connect to the internet.

All data you enter — including names, dates of birth, photos, events, and relationships — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

How Your Data Is Used

Data you enter is used solely to provide the features of the app:

  • Displaying your people and their upcoming events
  • Generating local push notifications for important dates
  • Rendering the family tree and statistics views
  • Producing PDF exports and JSON backups at your request

None of this data leaves your device.

Photos

Profile photos you add are stored on-device only. They are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Notifications

Celebrations uses Apple's local notification system to remind you of upcoming events. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not require an internet connection or any data to be sent externally.

Backups

The app provides an optional JSON export feature for backing up your data. This file is created locally and only shared if you choose to share it (e.g. via AirDrop, email, or Files). We have no access to these files.

Your device's system backup (iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder) may include Celebrations app data as part of a full device backup. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Celebrations does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs in the app that collect or transmit data.

Children's Privacy

Celebrations is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
