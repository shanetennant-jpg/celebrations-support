About Celebrations

Celebrations is a personal family event tracker for iPhone and iPad. Keep track of birthdays, anniversaries, and other important dates for the people who matter most — all privately stored on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored? All data is stored privately on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.
How do I back up my data? Go to Settings → Export Backup to save a JSON file containing all your people and events. You can import this file at any time via Settings → Import Backup.
How do notifications work? Celebrations uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming events. You can enable or disable these in Settings → Notifications. All notifications are generated on your device — no internet connection required.
How do I add a person? Tap the + button on the People tab. You can add a name, photo, date of birth, and any number of events such as anniversaries or other special dates.
How do I add events to a person? Open a person's profile and tap Add Event. Choose from over ten event types including birthdays, anniversaries, graduations, and more.
How do I export a family tree? Navigate to the Family Tree tab and tap the export button to generate a PDF of your family tree.
Can I delete all my data? Yes. Go to Settings → Delete All Data. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
What iOS version is required? Celebrations requires iOS 17 or later and is optimized for iPhone and iPad.
